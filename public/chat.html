How to upload to Bluehost

  1. Log in to your Bluehost account at bluehost.com
  2. In the left sidebar, click "Advanced"
  3. Click "File Manager"
  4. Navigate to the public_html folder
  5. Upload all files from the ZIP into public_html:
    index.html goes directly in public_html
    The uploads folder (if any) goes inside public_html too
  6. Visit your domain — your site is live!
