Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need any technical skills?
Not at all. If you can have a conversation, you can build a website. Our AI handles all the coding — you just describe what you want.
How long does it take?
Most sites are finished in 15–20 minutes. That includes setting up hosting, uploading your content, and refining the design.
What do I need to get started?
Just your photos, an idea of what you want your site to look like, and about 10 minutes to set up hosting. We guide you through everything else.
Is this really free?
The AI website builder is 100% free — no trial, no credit card, no catch. The only cost is web hosting, which every website on the internet requires (even ones built with Wix or Squarespace — they just hide it in their monthly fee). We've partnered with Bluehost to get you hosting starting at a few dollars a month with 60% off. That means you're getting a professional AI-built website for under $36/year — compared to $200–$400/year on other platforms.
Why do I need hosting?
Think of it this way: the AI builder creates your website's files, and hosting is the place where those files live so anyone can visit your site. It's like the difference between writing a book and putting it on a shelf in a store. Every website in the world needs hosting — we just make it easy and affordable.
What if I want to make changes later?
You own all the code. You can come back and use the builder again, or edit the files yourself. There's no lock-in.
What kind of sites can I build?
Business sites, portfolios, church websites, personal blogs, landing pages, restaurants — any standard website. It's best suited for single-page and small multi-page sites.
